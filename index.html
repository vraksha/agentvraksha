Every AI conversation starts as a blank slate. Vraksha reconstructs your technical state by injecting a three-tier memory architecture into every reasoning loop.

01
rules.md

the immutable ego

Hardcoded directives and behavioral constraints. Vraksha is physically barred from modifying this, ensuring your core logic is never hallucinated away.

02
memory.yaml

autonomous synthesis

Session-aware memory that auto-compresses key takeaways. We use mem0-inspired logic to retain high-fidelity facts without token bloat.

03

live agentic roadmap

Active projects, their technical state, and automated next steps. Vraksha always knows what you're building and where you left off.
